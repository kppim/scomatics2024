Call for Papers

i-SCOMATICS2024 serves as a platform for sharing and collecting high-quality and high-impact project developments by academic staff and students, highlighting their scholarly research in the fields of computer sciences and mathematics. The symposium provides an opportunity for participants to commercialize, promote, and showcase their research ideas and products to academics and researchers from within and outside UiTM. It also intensifies research and idea-sharing activities in addressing current issues in the higher education sector, industry, community, and alumni. Furthermore, i-SCOMATICS2024 encourages participants to elevate their research outcomes through high-impact publications, thereby broadening and enriching the world of publishing and knowledge sharing at a higher level.

The objectives of i-SCOMATICS2024 are as follows:
  • To provide a platform for academics and students to showcase their scholarly research, innovations, and technological advancements in the fields of Computer Sciences and Mathematics.
  • To empower scholarly research projects through the sharing of studies and ideas that address current issues in higher education institutions, industry, communities, and alumni networks.
  • To produce high-impact publications to further enrich the academic landscape and foster the exchange of knowledge in Computer Sciences and Mathematics.
  • To encourage collaboration and partnerships at various levels amongst national and international universities, industry, communities, and alumni, to enhance the collective expertise and progress in these fields.

i-SCOMATICS2024 is open to academics and students (Final Year Students and Postgraduate Students) in the fields of Computer Sciences and Mathematics. Participation is open to all UiTM branches and local universities in Peninsular Malaysia, Sabah, and Sarawak. The symposium will be held online on 24 October 2024. See you there!

Call for Reviewers

i-SCOMATICS seeks for new reviewers.

Reviewers play an important role in i-SCOMATICS publishing. The single blind peer review has been practised to:

  • Act as a validation system for all academic manuscripts submitted to the symposium.
  • Assist the organizer to maintain and improve the quality and integrity of published academic manuscripts.
  • Expand the research community network.

If you are interested, kindly provide your information in the Reviewer Application Form : https://forms.gle/srvWLt82R4aPdp5j7

Participants

All presentations will be done via online platform. Each presenter will be given 10 minutes of presentation (pre-recorded video) and 5 minutes of Question and answer session. Please refer to the presentation guidelines below.

Presentation Guidelines

  1. Record a 10-minute video presentation where you present in front of a camera. You can incorporate any innovative techniques to enhance your presentation. Consider using two screensâ€”one to display your PowerPoint slides and another to show yourself as the speaker.
  2. Presenters should ensure their face is clearly shown in the video recording.
  3. The video should be a maximum of 10 minutes long. After the video, there will be a 5-minute live Q&A session, and the presenter must attend this session.
  4. Organizer will provide virtual background. Participants are encouraged to use the background for the related events.
  5. The video should be in MPEG/MP4 format and have high resolution (720p or 1080p)
  6. Include all pertinent information, such as the title of your article, the names of the authors, their affiliations, and your photograph.
  7. Presentations must be given in English.
  8. The audio commentary and slide content must be presented clearly and in an easy-to-understand manner.
  9. There is no background music permitted.
  10. The video may be sent using the provided URL: https://forms.gle/ig9UdLWqrJ5W3J3z5

Submission Guidelines

Please follow these comprehensive guidelines to ensure your work is properly reviewed and considered for publication.

  1. The full paper must be submitted in English, using the provided Microsoft Word template.
  2. Author is suggested to proofread the paper. It is crucial to carefully check the scientific content and correct any grammatical errors.
  3. Your paper should be original, unpublished, and not under review elsewhere.
  4. Submit the paper via this link.
