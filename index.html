Call for Papers

SCOMATICS2024 serves as a platform for sharing and collecting high-quality and high-impact project developments by academic staff and students, highlighting their scholarly research in the fields of computer science and mathematics. The symposium provides an opportunity for participants to commercialize, promote, and showcase their research ideas and products to academics and researchers from within and outside UiTM. It also intensifies research and idea-sharing activities in addressing current issues in the higher education sector, industry, community, and alumni. Furthermore, SCOMATICS2024 encourages participants to elevate their research outcomes through high-impact publications, thereby broadening and enriching the world of publishing and knowledge sharing at a higher level.

The objectives of SCOMATICS2024 are as follows:
  • To provide a platform for academics and students to showcase their scholarly research, innovations, and technological advancements in the fields of Computer Science and Mathematics.
  • To empower scholarly research projects through the sharing of studies and ideas that address current issues in higher education institutions, industry, communities, and alumni networks.
  • To produce high-impact publications to further enrich the academic landscape and foster the exchange of knowledge in Computer Science and Mathematics.
  • To encourage collaboration and partnerships at various levels, including universities, industry, communities, and alumni, to enhance the collective expertise and progress in these fields.

SCOMATICS2024 is open to academics and students (Final Year Students and Postgraduate Students) in the fields of Computer Science and Mathematics. Participation is open to all UiTM branches and local universities in Peninsular Malaysia, Sabah, and Sarawak. The symposium will be held online on 24 October 2024. See you there!

Submission Guidelines

Please follow these comprehensive guidelines to ensure your work is properly reviewed and considered for publication.

  1. The full paper must be submitted in English, using the provided Microsoft Word template.
  2. Author is suggested to proofread the paper. It is crucial to carefully check the scientific content and correct any grammatical errors.
  3. Your paper should be original, unpublished, and not under review elsewhere.
  4. Submit the paper via this link.
